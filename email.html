Greetings from JSS College of Pharmacy, Ooty!

We are delighted to announce the Pharmanecia 4.E International Research Conference and invite all to submit research papers for this prestigious event.

Conference Highlights

  • Peer-reviewed paper publications and presentations
  • Keynote sessions and panel discussions
  • Focus on AI and ML in drug discovery
  • Intergenerational and interdisciplinary research platform
  • Oral and poster sessions
  • Awards ceremony for best papers, posters, and hackathon teams
  • Gala dinner and cultural performances

Key Topics

  • AI and ML driven drug discovery
  • Breaking barriers in Pharmaceutical Research
  • Natural Products for Drug Discovery
  • Targeted drug delivery systems
  • Inclusive Pharmaceutical Education & Research
  • Future of Pharmacy Education & Research
  • Standardisation of Phytopharmaceuticals
  • Gene and cell therapy approaches
  • Natural products in modern healthcare
  • AI in regulatory operations
  • Nanotechnology & Nanomedicine
